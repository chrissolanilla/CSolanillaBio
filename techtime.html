Tech Time

What is Tech Time?

Tech Time events are presented by the Techrangers team of the Center for Distributed Learning. They are presented to demonstrate the "nuts and bolts" functions of various computer-related applications. These sessions target a variety of audiences from UCF faculty and students to the general public. These are hands-on, in-depth sessions held in the Center for Distributed Learning offices or the Faculty Multimedia Center. Seating is limited so you must RSVP if interested.
