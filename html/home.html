The Techrangers is a team of students at the Center for Distributed Learning, who provide course development for faculty and web/application development for the University of Central Florida (UCF). The Techrangers act as student advocates and leaders by holding community workshops, participating in panels, and presenting at conventions.

Feel free to contact us, read about us, learn from us, and possibly join us!
